Privacy Policy for Project 4:6

Effective Date: 05/23/2025

Project 4:6 ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy outlines what data we collect and how it is handled. We are committed to not collecting any personal or sensitive data unless explicitly needed for app functionality.

1. Information We Collect

a. Local-Only App Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user-generated content. All prayer entries, reflections, journal notes, or media you create within the app are stored only on your device.

b. In-App Purchase Data via RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage purchases. RevenueCat may collect the following anonymous data for purchase validation only:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and install ID
  • Anonymous user identifier (generated by RevenueCat)
  • Purchase metadata (product ID, transaction date, price, currency)

We do not send any personal identifying information to RevenueCat.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Data Type Used For Stored Where
Prayer/journal content Displayed to you in the app On your device
RevenueCat purchase data Validating purchases and entitlements RevenueCat cloud (anonymous)

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following service for required functionality:

  • RevenueCat – In-app purchase verification and entitlement management. View their privacy policy

4. Data Sharing and Tracking

  • No data is shared with advertising or analytics platforms.
  • No tracking mechanisms or third-party cookies are used.
  • RevenueCat data is used only for app functionality.
  • No data collected is linked to your identity.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for users aged 13 and older. No personally identifiable data is collected from any user.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device. If you uninstall the app, your personal prayer data is deleted unless you have manually backed it up (e.g., iCloud or device backup).
